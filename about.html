Hi, I'm Ehsan! I'm a music producer, mixer, and engineer. I create fresh, unique songs from my studio based in Rochester, New York. I love all kinds of music and have a passion for making lots of it – from old school to future-sounding and everything in between.
I began composing and recording when I started college and have been experimenting with new sounds ever since.
Over the last couple of years, I’ve taken my music from a hobby to a full time career.
I work with recording artists, and other clients to help create and fulfill their musical dreams!
My intent is to create music that stands out, and speaks to emotion. I hope you’ll spend some time exploring the website, please get in touch!
